Dialect notes · show Bizkaian forms
tap a person — watch izan agree
The pattern: the verb izan changes shape for each person — naiz, zara, da, gara, zarete, dira. Unlike English ("I am, you are, he is"), the person word itself is often dropped, because the verb already tells you who. Learn the six verb forms and you can speak.
